How to Play

  • 1Pick a difficulty — Easy, Medium, or Hard to generate a puzzle
  • 2The timer starts automatically — fill in the grid!
  • 3Click a cell and type a number (1–9) or use Delete to erase
  • 4Wrong entries are highlighted in red automatically
  • 5Use arrow keys to navigate between cells
  • 6Stuck? Use 💡 Hint to reveal one correct cell
  • 7Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y to undo / redo. ⏸ Pause to pause the timer.
