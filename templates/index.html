How to Play Sudoku

The Goal

Fill every empty cell with a number from 1 to 9 so that no number repeats in any row, column, or 3×3 box.

The Three Rules

  • →
    Every row must contain the numbers 1–9 with no repetition.
  • ↓
    Every column must contain the numbers 1–9 with no repetition.
  • ⊞
    Every 3×3 box must contain the numbers 1–9 with no repetition.

Controls

  • 1–9 Type to fill a cell
  • Del Erase a cell
  • ↑↓←→ Navigate cells
  • Ctrl+Z Undo last move
  • Ctrl+Y Redo last move

Tips

  • 🔵 Blue cells are given clues — they can't be changed
  • 🔴 Red cells contain a mistake — fix them!
  • 🟡 Amber cells share a number with the selected cell
  • 💡 Use Hint to reveal one correct answer
